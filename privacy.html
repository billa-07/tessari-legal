Tessari — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 5, 2026

Tessari ("the app") is a habit tracking application published by Rajpoot Holding LLC ("we", "us"). This policy explains what information the app handles, where it is stored, and the choices you have. The short version: Tessari is local-first — your habit data stays on your device unless you choose to create an account and sync.

1. Information stored only on your device

By default, everything you create in Tessari is stored locally on your device and never leaves it:

  • Habits (names, icons, colors, schedules, categories, reminder times)
  • Completion history, streaks, notes, and achievements
  • Journey progress
  • App settings (theme, language, onboarding answers such as your usual wake and end-of-day times and goal)

If you never sign in, we have no copy of this data and cannot access it. Deleting the app deletes this data.

2. Information we collect if you create an account (optional)

Tessari offers an optional account for cloud sync (a paid Pro feature). If you sign up, we collect and store:

DataPurposeWhere
Email address and a hashed password Account authentication Supabase (our database provider)
Your habit and completion data Syncing your data across your devices and restoring it if you reinstall Supabase, linked to your account and protected so only your account can read it

We do not sell this data, use it for advertising, or share it with third parties except the service providers listed below. You can use the entire app without an account; in that case none of this applies.

3. Purchases

Subscriptions and one-time purchases are processed by Google Play. We use RevenueCat to manage subscription status. RevenueCat receives an anonymous purchase identifier and transaction details from Google Play; we do not receive or store your payment card details. See RevenueCat's privacy policy and Google's privacy policy.

4. Notifications

If you set habit reminders, the app schedules local notifications on your device. Reminder times are stored with your habits. Notifications are generated on-device; no notification data is sent to us.

5. What we do NOT collect

  • No advertising identifiers, no ad networks, no ads
  • No analytics or behavioral tracking SDKs
  • No location data
  • No contacts, photos, files, microphone, or camera access
  • No sale or sharing of personal data with data brokers

6. Service providers

We rely on the following processors solely to operate the app:

  • Supabase — database and authentication for optional accounts/sync
  • RevenueCat — subscription management
  • Google Play — app distribution and payment processing

7. Data retention and deletion

Local data lives on your device until you delete habits or uninstall the app. If you have an account, your synced data is retained until you delete your account. You can delete your account and all associated cloud data from within the app (Me → Account) or by emailing us; deletion removes your habit data and account records from our systems.

8. Children

Tessari is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, contact us and we will delete it.

9. Security

Synced data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS) and protected by row-level access controls so that only your authenticated account can read your data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we design the app so the minimum possible data leaves your device.

10. Your rights

Depending on your region (e.g., GDPR in the EU, similar laws elsewhere), you may have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data. The app's export feature lets you download your data at any time, and account deletion removes cloud copies. For any request, contact us at the address below.

11. Changes to this policy

If we change this policy, we will update the date above and, for material changes, note it in the app or the store listing.
